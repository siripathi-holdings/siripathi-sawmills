Our Story

Built on Decades of Craft

Since the early 1970s, Siripathi Saw Mills has been a cornerstone of Sri Lanka's timber industry. What began as a humble operation has grown into a trusted name across the island — built on honest work, fair pricing, and a genuine passion for quality wood.

Our seasoned taskforce brings decades of combined expertise to every log, every cut, and every order. Whether you need precision wood cutting services or bulk supplies of timber, firewood, or sawdust, we treat every customer like a long-standing partner.

We believe quality shouldn't cost more — and after more than half a century in business, we know how to deliver both.
